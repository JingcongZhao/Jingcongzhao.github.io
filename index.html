A bit about my professional background

I am currently attending a two-months, intensive training program on developing core skills in technical marketing to be a high impact employee at a startup.

Previously, I worked as an associate consultant at Communispace, a consumer collaboration agency, and focused on getting consumers to collaborate with companies, and finding actionable insights that help brands re-envision their products, services and brand experience. I got to work with a range of Fortune 500 companies as clients as well as many types of people who became members of our online communities.

Previous to Communispace, I worked in PR firms and research and advocacy firms with a focus on corporate social responsibility and sustainable business practices.
